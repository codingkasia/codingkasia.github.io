About

My name is Kasia Rosenberg and I am a full stack software engineer proficient in Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, and React. In a rare combination, I bring to the table not only a well-rounded foundation of programming skills, business acumen, and keen sense for design but also strong client relationship and engagement skills. While my professional experiences are evident in my Work History and Portfolio sections I would like to emphasize some qualities that cannot be conveyed as easily: grit, perseverance and work ethic. I grew up in a tiny village in communist Poland. Finding few opportunities in the poverty of post-Communist Poland, I moved to the States. Moving to a country with a different culture, without family or friends, and where the language is new was very challenging. However, while working I studied English intensively and mastered it well enough to be accepted to Johns Hopkins University. While at John Hopkins as a full time student, I also worked full time to pay for my living and educational expenses . The most valued skills I mastered during that time was an ability to prioritize tasks and balance various responsibilities while keeping focus on my goals. I was able to complete a four year degree in less than three years, graduating Magna Cum Laude. Having lived and worked in three countries has helped me develop exceptional communication skills and the ability to work with many diverse kinds of peoples. I am looking for more than a job; I'm looking for a career where I can be challenged, thrive, and never stop learning and growing.

Contact me if you believe your company and I would be a great fit!
