About

My name is Kasia Rosenberg and I am a full stack software engineer with experience in Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, and React. In a rare combination, I bring to the table not only a well-rounded foundation of programming skills, business acumen, and keen sense for design but also strong client relationship and engagement skills, having led communications with internal and external stakeholders. While my professional experiences are evident in my Work History and Portfolio sections I would like to emphasize some qualities that cannot be conveyed as easily: grit, perseverance and work ethic. I grew up in a tiny village in communist Poland, with one grocery store. My dad had to drop out of school at 15 to work, and I was one of few people in my village to go to a university. I graduated with honors from Jagiellonian University, which includes Nicolaus Copernicus and Pope John Paul II among its notable alumni. Finding few opportunities in the poverty of post-Communist Poland, I moved to the States. Moving to a country with a different culture, without family or friends, and where the language is new was very challenging. However, while working I studied English intensively and mastered it well enough to be accepted to Johns Hopkins University. While at John Hopkins as a full time student, I also worked full time to pay for my living and educational expenses . The most valued skills I mastered during that time was an ability to prioritize tasks and balance various responsibilities while keeping focus on my goals. I was able to complete a four year degree in less than three years, graduating Magna Cum Laude. My professional experience includes working as a research analyst for CEB now Gartner, in London, a globally esteemed business research and advisory firm, and also years as an entrepreneur in Washington, D.C., having founded two sole-proprietor businesses to deliver technology solutions to various markets. Having lived and worked in three countries has helped me develop exceptional communication skills and the ability to work with many diverse kinds of peoples. I am looking for more than a job; I'm looking for a career where I can be challenged, thrive, and never stop learning and growing. Contact me if you believe your company and I would be a great fit!
